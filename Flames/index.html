The Calgary Flames are my favourite hockey team and I will be listing my top 5 favourite players showing their career stats.

#5 Johnny Gaudreau

Johnny was a drafted by Calgary in 2011 and was with the organization until just this year in 2022 when we decided to sigh with the Columbus Blue Jackets. Despite the abrupt leave Johnny was still a great hockey player to watch as a Flame. He would amaze with his great puck handling skills and was always a threat to score.

Robyn Regehr was drafted in 1998 by the Colorado Avalance but was trade to Calgary prior to his professional career began. He was a tough player and was apart of the Flames in a far more physical era of the sport. He was tenacious and defended him teammates with great physical play and as such was a delight to watch steamroll oppenents.

#2 Jarome Iginla

Jarome Iginla was drafted 11th overall by the Dallas Stars but signed a contracted with Calgary in 1996 and made his debut in the playoffs. He became Captain of the team in 2003 and was with the team for 16 seasons. He was an exemplary player that excelled at all aspects of the game and was a true leader and represented Canada many times on the internation stage.

#1 Miikka Kiprusoff

Miikka Kiprusoff was drafted 116th overall in 1995 by the San Jose Sharks and was the starting goalie of the Flames when I watched my first season of hockey, he inspired me to become a goaltender myself and was a stoic presense for the stanley cup run of 2004. He was amazing to watch and truly in my opinion one of the greatest goalies to ever play for the Calgary Flames.
